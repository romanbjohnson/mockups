Your time and talent can make a real difference in people's lives.

Blood Donor Ambassador

Welcoming smiles needed! As a Blood Donor Ambassador volunteer, you will engage donors by greeting, registering, answering questions, providing information, and supporting them through the recovery process at the refreshments table. The special attention you would provide helps create a favorable impression that encourages ongoing donor support.

Blood Transportation Specialist Urgent need for volunteers

Join a lifesaving blood support team in your community.

As a Transportation Specialist volunteer, you will be the critical link between blood donors and blood recipients by delivering blood, platelets or other blood products to a hospital.
